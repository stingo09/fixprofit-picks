Starter repair kit

Starter repair tools for the first paid handyman jobs

A beginner handyman does not need a truck full of tools to start. The first kit should cover simple repair calls: hanging shelves, tightening hardware, installing small fixtures, assembling items, finding studs, measuring, drilling, and carrying anchors or screws neatly.

Best first rule: if a tool does not help finish the next paid job, wait.

When to upgrade
